# ADR-003: Scope Expansion for Comprehensive Personal Data Ingestion

## Status

Proposed

## Context

The `own-garmin` project's initial scope was limited to Activity-based data (Activities, Activity Details, and FIT files). While these provide high-fidelity GPS and sensor data for specific events, they represent only a fraction of the total biometric and lifestyle data generated by the Garmin ecosystem.

To transform `own-garmin` into a true "Personal Data Lakehouse," we need to ingest 24/7 health metrics that are not contained within workout-specific FIT files. This requires moving beyond activity endpoints to encompass wellness, physiological, and device-status endpoints.

## Decision

We will expand the data extraction scope of `own-garmin` to include all accessible health and wellness metrics. The expansion will be categorized into the following functional domains:

1. **Wellness & Recovery:**
    * **Sleep:** Durations, sleep stages (REM, Deep, Light), and Sleep Score.
    * **Body Battery:** Daily energy levels and charge/drain cycles.
    * **Stress:** All-day stress levels and stress-free periods.
    * **HRV (Heart Rate Variability):** Overnight baselines and nightly averages.

2. **Daily Physiological Metrics:**
    * **Heart Rate:** Daily resting HR, maximum HR, and high-resolution all-day HR samples.
    * **Intensity Minutes:** Weekly totals and moderate/vigorous splits.
    * **Respiration:** Breaths per minute during sleep and throughout the day.
    * **SpO2 (Pulse Ox):** Blood oxygen saturation levels during sleep or spot checks.

3. **Physical Composition & Goals:**
    * **Weight & Body Composition:** Weight, BMI, body fat percentage, skeletal muscle mass, and hydration.
    * **Steps & Floors:** Daily totals, goal progress, and distance equivalents.

4. **Integration Strategy:**
    * **Incremental Backfill:** The system must allow for a "Historical Deep Dive" to pull years of data, followed by incremental daily syncs.
    * **Endpoint Mapping:** We will map internal endpoints to these domains (e.g., `/wellness-service/wellness/dailySummaryData` for steps/intensity and `/sleep-service/stats/sleep` for sleep metrics).

## Consequences

### Positive

* **Contextual Correlation:** Users can now correlate workout performance (from FIT files) with recovery metrics (Sleep/HRV) within the same DuckDB/Iceberg environment.
* **True Ownership:** Accomplishes the project goal of "owning" the full spectrum of data stored on Garmin's servers, not just training data.
* **Enhanced Reporting:** Enables the creation of "Year in Review" or long-term health trend dashboards that go beyond simple mileage counts.

### Negative

* **API Surface Growth:** Every new metric represents a potential point of failure if Garmin changes the underlying undocumented endpoint.
* **Storage Increase:** While JSON is relatively small, the "All-Day" high-resolution heart rate and stress data can grow the Bronze layer significantly over several years.
* **Extraction Duration:** A full historical sync for a long-time Garmin user will take significantly longer and requires more aggressive rate-limit management.
